## Based On

### QuakeSpasm Engine
- **Eric Wasylishen** (ericw)
- **Sander van Dijk** (sezero)
- **Stevenaaus**

QuakeSpasm is a modern, cross-platform Quake game engine based on FitzQuake.
Website: http://quakespasm.sourceforge.net/

### FitzQuake
- **John Fitzgibbons** (metlslime)

FitzQuake is an enhanced GLQuake engine with improved limits and features.
